FAQs

Answers to common questions.

If you need anything else, send a note and I will respond within two business days.

How far in advance should we book?

Most sessions are scheduled 2-4 weeks ahead. For events, 6-8 weeks is ideal.

Do you travel across Estonia?

Yes. Tartu is home base, but I regularly travel to Tallinn, Parnu, and beyond.

When will we receive the photos?

First selects arrive within 24 hours, final edits typically within 7-10 days.

Can you help with styling?

Absolutely. I can suggest simple styling, locations, and a moodboard.

How do we receive the files?

You will get a private online gallery with web and print-ready exports.

What is the payment schedule?

Booking is confirmed with a deposit. The balance is due upon delivery.
